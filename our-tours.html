Overview

All tours are tailor-made, planned based on the duration of your trip and desired destinations. Most scheduled trips are between 5 and 30 days, and span across one to multiple regions of Ethiopia. For example, you may book a 7 day trip only to Omo Valley to see the tribes. Alternatively, you can book a 2 week trip covering the North, South and Omo Valley. High season is during the Western holidays, between mid-December to end of February. The rest of the year is considerably less busier and allows for flexibility in lodging. The cost of the trip covers the following expenses:
  • Transportation and fuel
  • Domestic flights (when needed)
  • Hotel / Lodging
  • 3 fresh meals per day
  • Unlimited supply of bottled mineral water
  • Entry fees for every village and site
  • Local guide fees (required at each village)
  • Picture fees in special areas (such as the Omo Valley tribes)
*Costs do not include tips for main guides and site guides.
**Tour costs vary with duration, number of destinations, number of guests etc. To receive a quote, please fill out our request form and we will be in touch shortly.
